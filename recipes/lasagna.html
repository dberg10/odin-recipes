Lasagna Recipe

Dustin's famous Bison Lasagna

Here is a great lasgna recipe that is perfect for Fall weather.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Bison
  • Ground Pork
  • Ricotta Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook Ground Bison and Ground Pork together
  2. Add in Marinara Sauce & veggies
  3. Place Lasagna Noodles in Dish, followed by sauce and then the ricotta cheese. Repeat until dish is filled. Finish with Sauce on top and a layer of cheese.
