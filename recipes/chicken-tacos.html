Chicken Tacos Recipe

Dustin's famous Chicken Tacos

Here is a great chicken taco recipe that takes 30 minutes or less to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs
  • Juice of an orange
  • Juice of a lime
  • Grain Free Tortillas
  • Seasonings - Cumin, Chili Powder, Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Garlic Powder
  • Salsa of choice
  • Guacamole

Steps:

  1. Mix seasonings and sprinkle on chicken thighs in baking dish
  2. Add juices and honey
  3. Bake in oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. Remove chicken and shred
  5. Broil on high for 7 minutes
  6. Heat tortillas, add chicken, salsa, guacamole and any additional toppings of your choosing.
