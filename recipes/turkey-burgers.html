Quick and Easy Turkey Burgers!

Here is a quick way to make turkey burgers for the family in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Primal Kitchen Ranch
  • Dijon mustard
  • Avocado Oil

Steps:

  1. Mix together 2 tablespoons of Ranch and 1 tablespoon dijon per pound of Ground Turkey with the meat
  2. Form burger patties
  3. Heat avocado oil in pan on medium high heat
  4. Place patties on the pan and cover. Cook 5-7 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
